Alderr began with a simple but stubborn problem: healthcare billing was too confusing, too expensive, and too misaligned for patients, providers, and employers alike. After a decade of operating revenue-cycle organizations and supporting thousands of providers, our founders saw a path forward—one built on clean data, transparent incentives, and modern AI infrastructure. Today, Alderr is the platform unifying those principles into a single operating system for healthcare finance.

A Decade Inside the Revenue Cycle

Before Alderr existed, our team ran one of the largest independent billing and RCM organizations in the country. We worked alongside practices, hospitals, billing companies, and global BPO teams. We saw firsthand how good teams were held back by fragmented systems, unclear economic incentives, and workflows that required endless manual intervention. The insight was clear: alone wasn’t the issue—alignment was.

Platform Built on Alignment

Alderr was created to rebuild the financial infrastructure of healthcare around transparency and shared outcomes.

We designed activPractice, activWork, and activPatient as interconnected products that reduce complexity, enforce clarity, and help every stakeholder—providers, billers, patients, employers—operate with the same information and the same goals.

When incentives align, efficiency follows. And when efficiency rises, the cost of care falls.

Proven in Real Operations, Not Just Theory

Unlike traditional software built in isolation, Alderr’s tools were refined inside real production environments, serving real practices with real volume.

From front-desk accuracy to AI-assisted workflow routing to patient-ready statements, every feature has been stress-tested across millions of transactions.

The result is software that doesn’t guess how healthcare works—it reflects it.

What Guides Us

Our principles shape the platform, the products, and the partnerships we build.

Transparency as a Foundation

Clear data and honest reporting are prerequisites for trust—between patients, providers, and partners.

Alignment at Every Level

Our products are designed so incentives reinforce good outcomes, not obscure them.

Efficiency Through Intelligence

AI and automation should remove friction, not add complexity. Everything is built to make work lighter.

Fairness for Every Stakeholder

Billing should not favor size or sophistication. Small practices deserve the same clarity as large systems.

Meet the Founders

Operators, architects, and builders committed to rewriting the financial experience of healthcare.

Baptiste Barrandon

Co-Founder & CEO

Baptiste spent more than a decade running revenue-cycle organizations supporting practices, billing companies, and health systems across the U.S. and abroad. His work exposed the structural misalignments that drive cost and complexity throughout the system. Alderr’s product philosophy—alignment, clarity, and defensibility—comes directly from those lessons.

Sunil Kumar

Co-Founder & COO

Sunil has led large-scale operational teams delivering RCM services at global scale. His expertise in workflow design, exception management, and operational analytics shaped activWork’s architecture and ensures that Alderr’s products reflect real production constraints.

Ramesh R. / Sankar S.

Co-Founders, Engineering & Architecture

With decades of enterprise engineering and AI experience, Ramesh and Sankar built the technical backbone of the Alderr platform—single-source data models, workflow orchestration, and the multi-platform communication layer that ties activPractice, activWork, and activPatient into a unified ecosystem.
