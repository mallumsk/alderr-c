activWork | Multi-Org Architecture

A Flexible Architecture That Adapts to Your Entire RCM Ecosystem

activWork mirrors how real revenue cycle operations function. Build separate spaces for clients, vendors, partners, and internal teams—then connect them through shared queues, assignments, clarifications, and data flows. Every group works the way they need, while you maintain complete control.

Model Every Relationship in Your Network

RCM often spans multiple organizations—billing companies, coding firms, BPOs, auditors, and AI tools. activWork lets you represent each one cleanly, route work to them, collect clarifications, and maintain visibility without blending environments.

Seamless Workflows Across Vendors, Clients, and Internal Team

Send tasks to vendors, request missing data from clients, or assign work to specialists—all within a single system. Each organization receives only the work intended for them, while you keep a full picture of progress across the entire network.

Integrate Deeply or Keep It Light — Your Partners Choose

Some partners prefer full workflow participation; others only need a simple queue or lightweight SFTP handoff. activWork supports every level of engagement—from full multi-user integration to a single assignment queue—letting you work with vendors on their terms.

Embed AI and Automation Directly Into Your Ecosystem

AI tools, bots, and automated checks can operate as standalone organizations within activWork. Route exceptions, documentation reviews, or coding validations through AI the same way you would with a vendor—creating a transparent, auditable layer of automated support.
