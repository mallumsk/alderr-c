Role-Based Routing That Matches Work to Skill Sets

Not all tasks should go to every user. activWork directs items to the people best equipped to handle them, based on role, specialty, or experience. This ensures faster resolution, higher accuracy, and more predictable throughput.

Smart Escalation to Prevent Bottlenecks and Aging

When tasks approach deadlines or stall in a queue, activWork automatically escalates them. The system can reassign work, notify supervisors, or adjust priorities—keeping aging under control and preventing missed payer windows.
