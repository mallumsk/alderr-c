What If Billing Didn’t Have to Cost Anything?

For decades, billing has been treated as an expensive, unavoidable overhead. activPractice challenges that assumption. By rebuilding billing around clean data, transaction-level economics, automation, and patient activation, we make it possible for practices to run their revenue cycle at little—or even no—cost.

Clean Data Reduces the Real Cost of Billing

Most billing costs come from rework—fixing missing documentation, correcting errors, resolving denials, and chasing AR.

activPractice focuses on preventing these problems at the source:

  • structured charge capture
  • automated eligibility
  • integrated documentation
  • payer rule checks

When claims are clean the first time, the cost to process them drops dramatically. Free billing starts with clean billing.

Transactional Pricing Rewards Accuracy, Not Percentage Markups

Traditional billing models take a percentage of collections, regardless of quality.

activPractice’s per-claim model aligns incentives:

  • high accuracy = low downstream cost
  • strong first-pass resolution = less AR
  • fewer touches = lower total cost per claim

As the system gets cleaner and more efficient, the marginal cost per claim approaches zero—and we pass those savings back to the practice.

Button

Automation Handles What Used to Be Manual

Eligibility checks, documentation matching, coding validations, claim-status updates, and even simple AR tasks can be automated.

These automations reduce human labor dramatically, and activPractice integrates them directly into the billing workflow. As automation expands, billing becomes less about man-hours and more about precision systems—making free billing not just possible, but sustainable.

Button

The Transparency Dividend: When Practices Share Clarity, Costs Fall Faster

Practices choose activPractice to gain transparency.

When they share that clarity with patients—through clean statements and honest financial communication—the system becomes even more efficient:

  • fewer patient calls
  • fewer disputes
  • fewer escalations
  • faster resolution of balances

Every avoided friction point reduces operational cost. activPractice returns these savings directly to the practice as the Transparency Dividend —a monthly credit that grows as patient confusion declines. Over time, the Dividend can offset a meaningful portion of the billing cost, and for some practices, all of it.

A Roadmap to Free Billing

Every practice begins with transactional pricing. As accuracy improves, automation grows, AR decreases, and patient escalations fall, billing becomes lighter, faster, and cheaper. And as practices earn monthly Transparency Dividends for sharing clear, patient-friendly communication, cost drops even further. Many practices see meaningful cost reduction within months—and some ultimately reach a true zero-cost model.
